Hard Puncher

Drum and bass, punk rock based out of Denver, CO. I play drums and my friend Manny Black plays bass.

Cougar Gold

Alt/punk project out of Denver I played drums for. Good guys.

Archway - Upbeat pop-punk song about the archway in Nebraska.
Matter Much - One of the first songs I learned upon joining the band.

Dagger van Gogh

Hard rock, kind of like Nirvana. I played drums.

Great Oscillator - This song was played a few times on KROQ in Los Angeles.
