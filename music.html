Chris Moore's Music Portfolio

The Bentleys 2007-2012

Mod Rock inspired by The Beatles. I was their third drummer.

Great Oscillator - This song was played a few times on KROQ in Los Angeles.
B Sharps - First song I played with the band.
For the Masses - One of their older songs I re-did the drums for.

1000 Needles 2010-2012

Bit-rock chiptune band from the San Fernando Valley. I played guitar. Wrote some songs. I left the band because I had to move to Denver, CO for work.

Inferno - I play guitar here.
Haymaker - I played guitars and wrote the drums.

Dagger van Gogh 2003-2008

Hard rock, kind of like Nirvana. I played drums.

Seattle - One of the first songs written by the band.
Homo Erectus - About a homophobic homosexual.
You are What You Ate - Song about rich people.

Hard Puncher / Comic Book Logic 2001-Present

Drum and bass, mostly instrumental, punk rock based out of Palmdale, CA. I play drums and my friend Manny Black plays bass. We both write the songs. Never played a show, more of a personal side project.

Big Trouble in Little China - Big Kurt Russell fan.
E Chuta - From Star Wars.
In the Shadow - Based on the PS2 game "Shadow of the Colossus." Incomplete bridge (only drums).
